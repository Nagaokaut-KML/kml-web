Hobby

読書

I like to read from manga to american novels. As far as genre goes, I prefer the fantasy and sci-fi after that. Although I had reading as a hobby for quite some time, I haven't read a large quantity of titles as I read mostly when commuting or before sleeping. At the moment, I am enjoying the beginning of the "Song of Ice and Fire" series by George R. R. Martin. If you want to learn about the prehispanic culture of Mexico, I recommend you to read a historical fiction novel titled "Azteca" by Gary Jennings.
